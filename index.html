About Me

My name is Emma, i'm a florist from Melbourne.

I am passionate about sustainability and using local flowers. I beleive that floristry is showcasing what nature has to offer, so in return I think we should take care of our natural world.

I am also completely foam free in my floral designs. Floral foam is a common product used in weddings and events, but has consequences on our planet, and our health. Read more about that here!
